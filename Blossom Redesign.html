Naming candidates
NAMES TO CONSIDER
What should we call it?
Blossom
Your current. Sweet, but generic — there are ~12 productivity apps with this name on the App Store.
Mochi
Name the app after the pet. Warm, unique, easy to say. The whole brand becomes "your friend Mochi".
Fernlight
Editorial-feeling. Hints at slow growth + a quiet evening. Pairs well with the journal aesthetic.
Tendr
● ACTIVE
"Tend to your tasks" — caretaker verb, not a flower. Modern handle, plays well as a domain.
Posy
Tiny, charming. A posy is a small bunch of flowers — implies you collect little wins.
Daybook
Most editorial — the app IS a daybook. Frames the product, not the metaphor.
My pick: Mochi if you want the brand to be the pet, or Daybook if you want it to feel like serious journaling software.
